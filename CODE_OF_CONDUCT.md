# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents to the maintainers via repository issue or security contact channel.
